# AIEC

## The System of Record for Enterprise AI Governance

AIEC enables organizations to create verifiable governance records for AI-generated content, human approvals, compliance reviews, and audit preparation workflows.

As AI adoption accelerates across enterprises, organizations face a growing challenge:

AI can generate content in seconds.

Governance takes weeks.

AIEC eliminates Governance Debt by establishing a tamper-evident Evidence Chain that tracks the complete lifecycle of AI-assisted work—from generation to approval to audit export.

> If it isn't in the Evidence Chain, it didn't happen.

---

## Why AIEC Exists

Modern enterprises are rapidly deploying AI across:

* Legal Operations
* Financial Reporting
* Customer Support
* Software Development
* Risk Management
* Internal Knowledge Systems

However, existing tools were never designed for AI governance.

Organizations today rely on:

* Email approvals
* Slack conversations
* Screenshots
* Word documents
* Wikis
* Manual evidence collection

As AI usage scales, these workflows become increasingly difficult to audit, govern, and defend.

AIEC creates a verifiable system of record that bridges the gap between AI generation and human accountability.
